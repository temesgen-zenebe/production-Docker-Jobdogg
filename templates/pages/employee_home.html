FAQ'S

Why should I build my Profile on Jobdogg.com?
You are not just a piece of paper or words on a resume. You have a real identity on Jobdogg. Your unique profile and video resume give employers a deeper visibility of who you are and what separates you from the other “cats” out there.
What type of Jobs are offered on Jobdogg?
Jobdogg offers temporary, temp-perm, full-time, part-time, seasonal and permanent placements to include Internship opportunities, in all skill-sets, all industries and in every environment. If you are looking for something quick to make some fast bucks or looking for a long term career we got you covered.
Why do I need to do a Video?
Jobdogg gets it! It’s not scripted, not a ton of questions to respond back to with limited time, no nervous interviews, no mess-ups, just be “YOU”. Tell our employers your great “tricks”, be creative, be comfortable, relax and show off your K-9 instincts of how cool you are and re-record if you want to.
Why do I need to do a Background Check?
Employers require a background check and we increase your likelihood of getting hired by having this already done.
Why do I need to give my Social Security Number and upload my Social Security card?
Jobdogg will be processing your payroll and we need to ensure we are paying the right person.
What is E-Verify and why do I need to complete an I-9 Form and upload the required documents?
We need to make sure you are legally eligible to work in the U.S.
I am here on a H1 Visa can I still register with Jobdogg?
Of course, and Yes, although your status is temporary here in the United States, Jobdogg can assist with permanent residency and permanent work status. Jobdogg also employs candidates Internationally and Globally.
Why do I Need to Fill out a W-4 Form?
This tells Jobdogg how many deductions you are taking for payrolling purposes, and to ensure you receive your W-2 at the end of the year for tax filing purposes.
Why do I need to give my Drivers’ License?
A Drivers’ License is another form of identity that we require, so that we can verify you are who you say you are and ensure no one else is using your identity.
Why do I choose a Pay Option?
Jobdogg wants to ensure you are paid on-time and make it convenient for you. Choosing a pay option tells Jobdogg how you want to get paid every week.
Why is there a Safety Video & Quiz?
Jobdogg's employers want to ensure your safety as well as others while you are working on an assignment.
Why do I have to take a skill-set test?
There is no pass or fail, skill-set tests allow our employers to hire you faster knowing you possess the required skills.
Do I have to choose a Ride Share option?
Not at all. This is only if you need it and for your convenience. If you need a ride and you don’t have the money to pay, Jobdogg can handle that, but the cost will be billed back to you as an incidental and taken out of your payroll. (Rideshare option is only for transportation to and from your work assignment. Jobdogg will not pay and/or reimburse outside of your work assignments).
